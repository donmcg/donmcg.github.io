0. 0.25" Brass Square Bar 360-H02 Extruded

1. Fly cut flatten surface ~3/4" at end of bar for final thickness of 0.10". 

2. Cut lateral pocket 0.125" wide 0.020" deep, outer edge > 0.10" from bar end.

3. Advance tool 0.440" towards heel.
   Cut 2nd lateral pocket 0.125" wide 0.020" deep,
	 edge 0.020" from hole center = 0.315" from 1st pocket.

4. Square off bar end 0.060 from outer edge of lateral pocket.

5. Drill centered 0.125" (1/8") dia hole 0.125" from pocket inner edge.

6. Chamfer outer edge of pocket

7. Turn over. Chamfer hole to 0.09" dia. opening; check head of screw fit.

8. Rotate 90deg and cut shoulder.  rotate 180deg and cut other shoulder.

8. Cut off key.
