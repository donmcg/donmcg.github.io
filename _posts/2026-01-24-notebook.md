**From a message to Brulen:**

A year ago, plus, I ordered and received the Screw-Guide key SKU SM103081. 
Thank you, I received the parts with no problems... until
I finally got around to putting the screw into the hole and ... it doesn't fit.
 
The part you sent me is labeled "Tri Taptite CSK Screw" M3X8MM.

The screw that came out of the old HIF6 (HIF44?) carb is a bit longer and has a split end for locking in place. 
The old screw has a slot head, the split end, and the thread appears to be a bit more coarse than M3.
In fact, I find that a 4-40 screw fills the hole without complaint, but the fit seems a bit loose.
 
**Their reply:** was that the HIF6 is imperial and the HIF44 is metric.
The BSW 1/8-40 imperial screw is no longer available.

**From spec sheets:**

Metric:

- From J.C. Gupta and Sons

- M3x0.5 

- head diameter dk min 5.2mm, max 6.0mm

- (Measured head diameter of Brulen part: 5.5mm)

Imperial:

- From Bolt & Nut Australia

- BSW 1/8" countersunk head G316 stainless

- head diameter min 5.11mm, max 5.57mm
